Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form (available from school office or website)
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of birth certificate or passport
  • Previous school reports / transcripts
  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) results (for Senior 1 entry)
  • Recommendation letter from previous school (if applicable)
  • Entrance assessment / interview
  • Payment of registration and admission fees
